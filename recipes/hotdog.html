Hot dog... Literally just a hotdog

Moderate slappage depending on your mood

So yeah. This one is simple. This is for all you folks who really can't cook. It's just taking a hotdog and putting it in a microwave. If this is above your paygrade, just order take-out.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog
  • Hotdog bun
  • Whatever condiment you like

Steps

  1. First season your cast iron skillet. Lol jk. Make a small incision the length of the dog
  2. Put said dog on plate and microwave for 35-40 seconds
  3. Give that dog a bun
  4. Slap whatever condiments you want on it
  5. Throw it in the trash
